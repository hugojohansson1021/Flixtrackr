The Witcher Season 3 Release Date: Split Season

"The Witcher Season 3 Release Date: Split Season, Volumes, and Dates Discover the highly anticipated release date of The Witcher Season 3, set to captivate fans worldwide. This new season will be divided into two volumes, with Volume 1 premiering on June 29, 2023, followed by Volume 2 on July 27, 2023. Prepare for a thrilling journey with eight episodes, evenly distributed across the volumes. Filming for Season 3 concluded in September 2022, and with the easing of Covid restrictions, fans were eager for a late 2022 release. However, during Netflix's Tudum live stream, it was announced that The Witcher season 3 is scheduled for a summer 2023 launch. Showrunner Lauren Hissrich also confirmed this timeframe in a December 2022 interview with Collider, mentioning an estimated release window of "eightish months or so." Mark your calendars for the mesmerizing return of The Witcher in 2023! Stay updated for more news and prepare for an enthralling season of adventure and magic."
